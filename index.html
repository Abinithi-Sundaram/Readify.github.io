FIFTY SHADES OF GREY

E.L. James

"Fifty Shades of Grey" by E.L. James portrays Anastasia Steele's relationship with Christian Grey, delving into his controlling nature and their sexual dynamic, captivating readers with its explicit content and exploration of power dynamics.

IKIGAI

Hector García and Francesc Miralles

"Ikigai" by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles reveals the Japanese concept of finding purpose and happiness. Through anecdotes and research, it illustrates how embracing ikigai can lead to a fulfilling life of mindfulness and self-discovery.

IT ENDS WITH US

Colleen Hoover

"It Ends with Us" by Colleen Hoover follows Lily Bloom's journey as she navigates love and past trauma, confronting difficult choices and exploring themes of resilience, forgiveness, and self-discovery.
